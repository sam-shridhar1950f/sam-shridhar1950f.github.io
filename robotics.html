Hello and Welcome to 1261 Programming! We are super excited to have you guys slave away... *ahem* work diligently with us during this next year. I will be updating this page throughout the year to inform you guys of any programming specific news or assignments. Thank you for reading this!

Start of Year Assignment

  1. Create a GitHub account and share it with us (click here)
  2. Complete lab.github.com tutorial
  3. Take a look at the MIT OCW resources. We will be using this course to teach you basic programming.
  4. Complete the following assignments from the OCW site:
    • GravityCalculator
    • FooCorporation
    • Marathon
    • Library
  5. Make a pull request in the shared GitHub repository for each assignment you do
  6. We will assess your assignments and provide feedback on each of them
  7. If you are in need of help, you can always email one of us ( Tejas , Ian , or Ava )
